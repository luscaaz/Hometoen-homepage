Visit Muriaé

Enjoy culture and parties in this vibrant city in the heart of Brazil.

Top three activities to do at Muriaé

Explore wonderful waterfalls

The city is surrounded by both beautiful mountains, and a huge number of waterfalls to be explored!

Get some historical knowledge

Muriaé has a great variety of museums, where visitors can get knowledge of the past, and also a glimpse of the future!

Lots of day and night activities

A vibrant, ever growing nightlife attracts people from all corners of the state, daytime activities are also present!
